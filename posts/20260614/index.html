manipulations vs thinking clearly#

demagogues and strongmen upended arrangements, institutions, and norms not by battering bodies but by manipulating minds. they exploited fear, hatred, and confusion just like the market exploits fear and greed. once those raw emotions are stirred, it will drive the market and people crazy.

the agents of mass manipulation are states, churches, and the capital. the hotbeds of manipulation are sex, race and politics, both personal and social.

unable to handle the uncertainties and the vicissitudes of life, human beings vacillate between unfounded hope and irrational fear. religious manipulators exploits this emotional tumult to their advantage.

we are finite creatures surrounded by the vast infinity, people, animals, plants, bugs, mountains, rivers, lands and stars.
